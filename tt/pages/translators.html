As with most of the open source projects the default language of Padre is English. As with any other technology we believe that removing as many obstacles from the user as possible will just make their life easier. Hence we encourage the translation of the Padre GUI (and later on that of the documentation) to other languages as well.

This page is here so the translators can write a few words about themselves and/or about Padre in their own languages. For every translator there is a link to the developers page with an English description of why are they involved in the project.

If you would like to get involved in the translation project please check out our page about translations.
